# Retail Customer Intelligence Platform

## Business Problem

Retailers collect millions of customer transactions every day but often struggle to transform this data into actionable insights.

The objective of this project is to build an end-to-end Customer Intelligence Platform capable of supporting marketing, merchandising, and customer relationship management.

## Business Objectives

1. Identify customers at risk of churn.
2. Predict future customer spending.
3. Segment customers based on purchasing behaviour.
4. Recommend products customers are likely to purchase.
5. Measure customer lifetime value.
6. Analyse promotional campaign effectiveness.
7. Build executive dashboards.
8. Deploy predictive models through REST APIs.

## Expected Business Value

- Increase customer retention
- Improve marketing ROI
- Personalize customer offers
- Increase basket value
- Improve cross-selling
- Reduce churn
